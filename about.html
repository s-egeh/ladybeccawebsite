Our Story

Lady Becca Ltd. was born from a simple observation: a critical gap between the logistics and hospitality services that were available and the quality that clients deserved.

Our founder, a seasoned professional with deep roots in Tema, witnessed firsthand the frustrations many faced—unreliable deliveries, inconsistent accommodation standards, and a general lack of customer-centric service. Determined to set a new benchmark for excellence, she established Lady Becca Ltd. not just as a business, but as a solution.

Our mission was clear from day one: to build an integrated service company founded on the principles of reliability, transparency, and unparalleled quality. We started with a single vehicle and a passionate commitment, and have since grown into a trusted name for clients across the Greater Accra Region and beyond.

Our Journey

2021: The Beginning

Lady Becca Ltd. is founded in Tema with a mission to revolutionize local logistics and transport services with a single, reliable vehicle.

2022: Expanding into Hospitality

Recognizing a parallel need for quality accommodation, we acquire and launch our first premium Airbnb property in Adentan, focusing on comfort and top-tier amenities.

2023: Fleet and Team Growth

Our logistics division expands, adding new vehicles to our fleet and growing our team of professional drivers and support staff to meet rising demand.

2024: Formalizing Excellence

We successfully register as a Limited Liability Company, solidifying our professional standing and commitment to long-term growth and corporate governance in Ghana.

Today: Your Trusted Partner

Lady Becca Ltd. is now a leading integrated service provider, known for our unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction across all our divisions.
